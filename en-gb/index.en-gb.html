About me

I obtained a MSc in Computer Science from Cergy-Pontoise University, France, and a MSc in Electronics from the French engineering school ENSEA both in 2016. I did my last-year internship at Vilmorin-Mikado (Limagrain Group) on computer vision and machine learning applied to disease detection on maize seeds. I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Vision and Deep Learning at CY Paris University under the supervision of Aymeric Histace (ETIS), David Picard (Ecole des Ponts ParisTech) and Edouard Klein (French National Police Forces).
